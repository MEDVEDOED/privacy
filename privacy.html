Privacy Policy – G&S Transportation LLC

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Vehicle and shipping information
  • Billing and payment data (via third parties)
  • IP address, browser info, and device data

2. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for functionality, analytics (Google Analytics), and advertising (Meta Pixel). You may disable cookies via your browser settings. Our cookie banner lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used to:

  • Provide auto transport services
  • Communicate with customers
  • Handle billing and invoicing
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Target and measure ad campaigns

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
  • Analytics providers (Google)
  • Ad platforms (Meta/Facebook)
  • CRM and internal tools

5. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries. We ensure compliance with data protection laws when transferring data internationally.

6. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to fulfill our services and legal obligations, typically no longer than 5 years unless otherwise required.

7. Security

We use encryption, secure access controls, and data minimization to protect your information. In case of data breaches, we notify affected users within 72 hours.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or request deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing
  • Submit complaints to a data authority

To submit a request, contact us at privacy@gstransportation.com

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child provided data, contact us.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may request disclosure of personal data collected, request deletion, or opt out of the sale of personal information. To submit a request, email owner@gstransportation.com.

11. GDPR Compliance

We respect GDPR rights of EU users, including consent, portability, and data erasure. Legal basis includes legitimate interest and user consent. We use SCCs for data transfers.

12. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. We recommend reviewing it regularly. The current version will always be posted at /policy.html.
